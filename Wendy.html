Wendy Leblanc-Arbuckle
is a renowned Pilates teacher and movement educator. The founder and director of the Pilates Center of Austin and the 3-CoreConnections@ Perspective, Wendy teaches at her studio in Austin, Texas and travels extensively to teach workshops and at conferences. Wendy is also a founding member of the Pilates Method Alliance and she served on the original delineation committee for the creation of the PMA Pilates Certification Exam.
