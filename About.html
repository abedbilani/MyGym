MY GYM

Built over 800 square meters,MY GYM is the ideal destination
for those wanting to experience a unique lifestyle
filled with health and wellbeing. Our vision is clear.
We want to become the leading fitness and gymnastics club in Lebanon.
The ideal location of MY GYM makes it the perfect end to a long day's
work and the right stop to a new start towards a healthy lifestyle.
MY GYM is the ideal Club and healthy hangout venue for the entire family.
MY GYM is committed to providing members with professional and personalized
guidance towards a healthy lifestyle.
Our experienced, passionate and friendly staff are ready to walk you through
the first steps on your journey to becoming a fitter you a New you!
